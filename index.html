About Baticadogs Foundation

We are a volunteer-led dog donation foundation under the name Baticadogs Foundation. We partner with local vets and shelters to provide medical attention, safe temporary homes, and adoption support. Everything we do is built around kindness and community.

Your donation supports meals, shelter repairs, and fast response rescue missions.
